A multilingual peer community connecting E-9 workers together (gachi).

An AI-powered multilingual community that links E-9 migrant workers across Korea by industry. Real-time AI translation lets workers from six countries share experiences regardless of nationality, pairing peer experiences (RAG) with official guidance (SOP) so workers can assess situations on their own.
